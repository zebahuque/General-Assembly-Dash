Prague, Czech Republic

I visited Prague during a high school spring break trip to Eastern Europe. My favorite spot in this city was at the top of the Astronomical Clock Tower. The skyline was visible enough to see rows and rows of Prague's signature red roofs!

Ontario, Canada

My family and I visitied Niagara Falls when I was young. We were able to get a hotel room that gave the best view overlooking the falls. To my surprise, Ontario is the cleanest city I have ever seen to this day!

California, US

California is a state full of so many attractions people can't help coming back, which is what my family has done. We have visited California on three separate occasions, my favorite attraction being Death Valley. I have never seen mountains or a salt basin in person, which made the visit much more awe-inspiring.
